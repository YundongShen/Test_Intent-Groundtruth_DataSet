**Test Object**
The `handleRequest` method of `{{ operation }}Handler`

**Test Goal**
Verify the correctness of the content and status returned by the handler in a simple success scenario.

**Test Activities**
1. Create an instance of `{{ operation }}Handler`, constructing an empty resource model and a corresponding `ResourceHandlerRequest`.
2. Call `handler.handleRequest(proxy, request, null, logger)`.
3. Assert that the response is not null, the status is `OperationStatus.SUCCESS`, `callbackContext` is null, `callbackDelaySeconds` is 0, `resourceModel` equals `desiredResourceState` in the request, `resourceModels` is null, `message` is null, and `errorCode` is null.
