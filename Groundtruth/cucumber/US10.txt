**Test Object**
**Expense Entry Interface and Total Investment Update Logic**

**Test Goal**
Verify that after registering an expense for a specified crop, this expense is associated with that crop, and the total investment amount for the crop is updated synchronously.

**Test Activities**
1. Prepare a crop.
2. Enter an expense of S/150, assigned to Potato.
3. Check if the expense has been saved to the database and associated with Potato.
4. Check if the total investment amount for Potato has increased by 150.

**Test Object**
**Report Functionality for Viewing Cumulative Expenses by Crop**

**Test Goal**
Verify that after a user selects a crop and enters the financial panel, they can see a summary table listing the date, concept, amount, and total investment amount for all expenses related to that crop.

**Test Activities**
1. Select a crop.
2. (Optional) Set the query time range.
3. Enter the financial panel for that crop.
4. Check if the report table displayed on the page includes "Concepto de gasto" and "Monto" for each expense.
5. Check if "Inversión total" and the correct cumulative total are displayed at the bottom of the table.
