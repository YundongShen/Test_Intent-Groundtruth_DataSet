- **Test Object**
Automatic Alarm System's Disease/Pest Detection and Notification Function

- **Test Goal**
Verify that when the system detects a potential disease or pest and the risk level exceeds a threshold, it can automatically send an alert to the user's mobile phone, reminding them to check the disease details.

- **Test Activities**
1. The system detects abnormal crop conditions through sensor data and crop images.
2. The system searches for and identifies specific diseases or pests.
3. When the risk level exceeds a preset threshold, a notification is triggered.
4. Check if the user's mobile phone received the alert.

- **Test Object**
Automatic Alarm System's Extreme Weather Warning Notification Function

- **Test Goal**
Verify that when the system receives an extreme weather forecast and the event is approaching, it can automatically send a preventative notification to the user, prompting them to take protective measures.

- **Test Activities**
1. The system receives weather forecast data containing extreme weather events. 
2. As a weather event approaches, the system triggers a notification.
3. Check if the user's phone has received the notification.
