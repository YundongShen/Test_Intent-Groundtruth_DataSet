**Test Object**
A rich text editor within an iframe

**Test Goal**
Verify that text can be entered and bolded in the iframe editor.

**Test Activities**
1. Open the page containing the iframe.
2. Locate the iframe, obtain its internal `body` element, and confirm its visibility.
3. Enter "Hello" in the editor and select all.
4. Click the bold button in the toolbar.
