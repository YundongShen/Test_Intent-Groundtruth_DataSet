**Test Object**
`POPULATE.POPULATE` function

**Test Goal**
Verifies that, under a normal distribution, the output x matches the input, the y shape is correct, and the mean and standard deviation are close to the preset values.

**Test Activities**
1. Construct a vector `x` from 0 to 99999.
2. Call `POPULATE.POPULATE` with the parameters `distribution="normal"`, `normal_mean=0`, and `normal_standard_deviation=1`.
3. Check that `res.x` and `x.v` are completely identical, and that `res.y.shape` is the same as `x.v.shape`.
4. Verify that the absolute value of the mean of `res.y` is less than 0.01, and the absolute value of the difference between the standard deviation and 1 is less than 0.01.
