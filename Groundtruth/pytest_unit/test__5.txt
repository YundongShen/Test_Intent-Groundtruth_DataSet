**Test Object**
The `CustomerManager.add_customer` method

**Test Goal**
Verify that adding a customer returns a result containing all expected fields.

**Test Activities**
1. Initialize CustomerManager using a test TinyDB instance.
2. Call `add_customer` and pass in the name, email, age, and country.
3. Check that the returned dictionary contains all fields: name, email, age, country, and id.
