**Test Object**
Combination of the `matchAllQuery`, `deleteDocument`, and `refreshRequest` methods of `QueryDAO`

**Test Goal**
Verify that after retrieving all documents, deleting them one by one and refreshing the index, the final full query result is empty, meaning all data has been cleared.

**Test Activities**
1. Call `dao.matchAllQuery()` to retrieve the list of all documents.
2. Iterate through the list, calling `dao.deleteDocument(doc.getId())` to delete each document.
3. Call `dao.refreshRequest()` to refresh the index.
4. Call `dao.matchAllQuery()` again and assert that the returned list is empty.
