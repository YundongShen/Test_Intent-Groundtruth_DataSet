**Test Object**
The `findPopulationInContinentFiltered` query method of `ContinentRepository`

**Test Goal**
Verify that the method correctly returns filtered population statistics for each continent.

**Test Activities**
1. Load test data using a `@Sql` script.
2. Call `continentRepository.findPopulationInContinentFiltered()` to retrieve the results.
3. Check that the results are not empty and contain all three continents.
4. Verify the Asian data: Total population, Urban population, Urban population percentage, Rural population, Rural population percentage.
5. Verify the European data: Total population, Urban population, Urban population percentage, Rural population, Rural population percentage.
