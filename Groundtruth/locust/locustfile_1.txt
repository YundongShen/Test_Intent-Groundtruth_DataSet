**Test Object**
Translation Service (Homepage `/` and Translation API `/api/translate`)

**Test Goal**
Verify that the translation service can respond normally to homepage requests under high concurrency and can handle translation requests for multiple target languages.

**Test Activities**
1. Each virtual user randomly waits 5 to 15 seconds before executing a task.
2. Access the homepage `GET /` at a low frequency.
3. Send POST requests to `/api/translate` at a high frequency, carrying a piece of English text, with the target language randomly selected from 8 languages.
4. Continuously execute the above operations to simulate mixed access by real users.
