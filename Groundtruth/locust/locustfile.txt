**Test Object** 
Backend APIs related to users, groups, and revenue sharing.

**Test Goal** 
Verify that these APIs can stably handle registration, login, information viewing, group operations, and revenue sharing requests under concurrent scenarios.

**Test Activities** 
1. Simulate a batch of users. Each user registers three accounts, and the token and ID of the last registered account are used for subsequent operations.
2. Create a group using the obtained token, add the three newly registered users to the group, and record the group ID.
3. During continuous execution, randomly perform the following actions: view profile, view group list, view specific group details, and initiate a revenue sharing request.
