**Test Object**
`App` Component

**Test Goal**
Confirm the functionality of clicking the "Add Item" button.

**Test Activities**
1. Render the `App` component.
2. Initially check that there are no list items on the page and that the text "no items yet" is visible.
3. Locate the "Add Item" button and click it.
4. Verify that the list item becomes one, with the content "Item 1", and that "no items yet" is no longer displayed.
