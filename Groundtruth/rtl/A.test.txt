`<A>` Component

**Test Goal**
Verify that when the component lacks the `href` attribute, the console outputs a warning.

**Test Activities**
1. Simulate the `console.warn` function.
2. Render `<A>click link</A>`.

**Test Object**
`<A>` Component

**Test Goal**
Confirm that the bound `onClick` callback is triggered correctly when the component is clicked.

**Test Activities**
1. Prepare a flag variable `io`, initialized to 0, and set to 1 by the `onClick` callback.
2. Render `<A data-testid="a.clickme" onClick={onClick}>click</A>`.
